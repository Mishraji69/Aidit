# Go-To-Market

## Ideal customer profile
- Seed to Series C startups with 10 to 250 employees.
- Engineering or product-heavy teams with multiple AI subscriptions.
- Budget owner is a founder, ops lead, or finance manager.

## Core pain points
- AI tooling sprawl and redundant subscriptions.
- Unclear plan tiers for small teams.
- Lack of benchmarks for API spend.

## Positioning
Deterministic, rules-based AI spend auditing with transparent savings logic. No black box AI pricing.

## Primary messaging
- "Know where your AI budget leaks in minutes."
- "Transparent savings math you can show finance."
- "Replace plan guessing with deterministic audits."

## Acquisition channels
- Founder and operator communities (Slack, Discord, LinkedIn groups).
- Partnerships with fractional CFOs and finance advisors.
- Content: AI spend benchmarks, plan comparison guides.
- Paid experiments: LinkedIn lead ads targeting ops and finance.

## Conversion path
Landing page -> Run audit -> See results -> Optional email -> Shareable report.

## Pricing hypothesis
- Free audit for single-time use.
- Paid tier for recurring monitoring, alerts, and team access.

## Launch plan
Week 1
- Publish landing page and demo results.
- Recruit 10 design partners for feedback.
- Collect 5 to 10 audits for baseline data.

Week 2
- Add recurring email report.
- Share a public benchmark blog post.
