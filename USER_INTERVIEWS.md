# User Interviews

## Interview goals
- Validate primary pain points around AI tooling sprawl.
- Learn how teams decide plan tiers and vendors.
- Identify willingness to pay for ongoing monitoring.

## Recruit targets
- 5 founders at seed to Series A.
- 3 engineering managers at 20 to 100 person teams.
- 2 finance or ops leads with tool budget ownership.

## Discussion guide
1. What AI tools are you paying for today?
2. Which tools feel redundant or underused?
3. How do you decide when to upgrade or downgrade plans?
4. How is AI spend reported to finance?
5. What would make a savings recommendation feel trustworthy?
6. Would a shareable report help you communicate internally?
7. What would you pay for continuous monitoring?

## Signals to capture
- Estimated monthly spend.
- Number of stakeholders involved in decisions.
- Current approval workflow.
- Response to deterministic vs AI-driven logic.
